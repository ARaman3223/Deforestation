Sustainable Solutions


  1. One logical solution is to curb felling of trees (Felling trees involves cutting live, standing trees). It can be done through powerful rules and laws that set in place to limit corporations from reaping the resources out of forests. Laws must be strict and must have strict consequences when broken.

    2. Can only cut down small trees fully and can only cut down branches off big trees.
    3. Must replant as they cut. 2 saplings per one tree cut down
