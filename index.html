Deforestation

is the permanent removal of trees to make room for something besides forest, including clearing the land for agriculture or grazing, or using the timber for fuel, construction, or manufacturing.

The impact of deforestation is detrimental, leading to climate imbalance, increase in global warming, soil erosion, and wildlife extinction.
